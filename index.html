Debut Single Out Nov 14

Welcome to the official site of Musidk — an independent artist and singer-songwriter. Known for heartfelt ballads and raw vocals, Musidk’s music captures the ache of memory, loss, and love.

The debut single “Gone by Tomorrow” is coming November 14, 2025.

Music

Musidk’s songs blend vulnerable lyrics with haunting melodies. The debut single “Gone by Tomorrow” marks the beginning of a series of releases leading into the first album, When the Silence Calls Out Your Name (2026).

Stay tuned — this is just the start.

About

Musidk is an independent artist whose music lives in the quiet moments — a voice cracked by emotion, lyrics that cut deep, and melodies that linger.

With a minimalist, melancholic sound, Musidk creates songs that resonate with listeners who crave honesty and intimacy in music. This is not about image — it’s about connection.

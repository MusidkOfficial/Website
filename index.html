About

Musidk is an independent artist whose music lives in the quiet moments — a voice cracked by emotion, lyrics that cut deep, and melodies that linger.

With a minimalist, melancholic sound, Musidk creates songs that resonate with listeners who crave honesty and intimacy in music.
